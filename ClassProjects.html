⋆*･ﾟ:⋆*･ﾟ Alan Turing ⋆*･ﾟ:⋆*･ﾟ

For this project, we had to choose from a list of significant people in computer science. My partner, Gabe, and I chose Alan Turing, often called the "father of computer science". He is known for his contributions to the idea of the personal computer, as well as his "Turing Test", which became the basis for AI. We made a Capcut video for our presentation and we also played a Kahoot we made with the class.

For this project, we had to choose from a list of programming languages and make a poster about it. Gabe and I chose C# because it's a popular language to develop video games on Unity, since we love video games. C# was used to make many popular games, such as Cuphead and Super Mario Run. Due to Super Mario Run, we themed the poster after Mario to make it eye-catching.
